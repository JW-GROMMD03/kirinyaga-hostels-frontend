Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hostel?
Browse hostels, click on one you like, then click "Book Now". The booking will be held for 1 hour for you to complete payment.
What is two-factor authentication?
It adds an extra layer of security. After enabling it in your settings, you'll receive a one-time code via email each time you log in.
How do I report a scam?
On the Roommate Finder page, each ad has a "Report Scam" button. Click it and provide details. Our admin team will review it.
I forgot my password – what should I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it via email.
