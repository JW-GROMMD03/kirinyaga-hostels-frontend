Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hostel?
Browse hostels, click on one you like, then click "Book Now". The booking will be held for 1 hour for you to complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once the booking is confirmed.
What is two-factor authentication?
Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. After enabling it in your settings, you'll receive a one-time code via email each time you log in, in addition to your password.
How do I report a scam or fraudulent listing?
On the Roommate Finder page, each ad has a "Report Scam" button. Click it and provide details about the suspicious activity. Our admin team will review and take appropriate action.
I forgot my password – what should I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address. The link expires in 1 hour.
How do I save a hostel to my favorites?
While viewing a hostel, click the "Save" button on the hostel card or detail page. Saved hostels can be accessed from your dashboard under "Saved Hostels".
How can I contact a hostel owner?
On the hostel detail page, you'll find the owner's contact information including phone number and email. You can reach out directly to inquire about availability or ask questions.
