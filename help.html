Getting Started

How do I add a new hostel listing?
To add a new hostel listing:
1. Click on "Add Hostel" from your dashboard
2. Fill in all required details (name, description, location, pricing)
3. Upload photos of your hostel
4. Submit for approval
5. Once approved, your listing will be visible to students

Approval typically takes 24-48 hours.
How long does hostel approval take?
Hostel listings are reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your listing is approved or if additional information is needed. You can check the status in your dashboard under "Manage Hostels".
What documents do I need to register as a hostel owner?
Required documents:
- National ID or Passport
- Business registration certificate
- County government license for accommodation
- Fire safety certificate
- Health inspection certificate
- KRA PIN certificate
Upload these during registration for verification.

Bookings

How do I manage booking requests?
You can manage bookings from your dashboard:
1. Go to "View Bookings"
2. See all pending and confirmed bookings
3. Click on any booking to view details
4. Confirm or cancel as needed
You'll receive email notifications for new bookings.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can set your own cancellation policy. Common options:
- Flexible: Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in
- Moderate: Free cancellation up to 14 days before check-in
- Strict: 50% refund up to 30 days before check-in
You can set this when creating or editing your hostel listing.
How do I receive payments from bookings?
Payments are processed through our secure payment system. Funds are released to your account 24 hours after guest check-in. You can view your payment history in the "Analytics" section. Withdrawals can be made to your M-Pesa or bank account.

Hostel Management

How do I update my hostel information?
To update hostel information:
1. Go to "Manage Hostels" from your dashboard
2. Click on the hostel you want to edit
3. Update the information (pricing, availability, photos, etc.)
4. Click "Save Changes"
Note: Major changes may require re-approval.
How do I respond to reviews?
To respond to reviews:
1. Go to "Reviews" from your dashboard
2. Find the review you want to respond to
3. Click "Reply"
4. Type your response and submit
Professional responses help build trust with potential guests.
How can I track my hostel's performance?
Use the Analytics section to track:
- Number of views
- Booking conversion rates
- Revenue trends
- Guest reviews and ratings
- Occupancy rates
This data helps you optimize your listing and pricing.

Account & Security

How do I change my password?
To change your password:
1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select "Edit Profile"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click "Save Changes"
Use a strong password with at least 8 characters, including numbers and symbols.
How do I enable two-factor authentication?
To enable 2FA:
1. Go to "Security" from your dashboard
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place
2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account.
What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?
If you suspect unauthorized access:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Enable 2FA if not already enabled
3. Contact support at security@kirinyaga-hostels.ac.ke
4. Review recent activity logs
5. Log out of all devices from security settings
We'll investigate and help secure your account.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble uploading photos. What should I do?
Common issues and solutions:
- Ensure images are JPG or PNG format
- File size should be less than 5MB
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- Clear your browser cache
- Check your internet connection
If issues persist, contact support with error details.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
To report technical issues:
1. Take screenshots of the issue
2. Note what steps led to the problem
3. Email support@kirinyaga-hostels.ac.ke
4. Include your browser and device information
5. We'll respond within 24 hours
